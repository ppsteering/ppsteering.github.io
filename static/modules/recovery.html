PPS recovers when a rollout goes wrong

Sometimes a rollout goes off track. The policy fumbles a grasp, misses a lid, or a person nudges the object mid-task. When that happens, PPS simply tries again, even though the demonstrations never show a single recovery. LoRA learned the demonstrations too literally, so it keeps going and the task fails.
